**TASK 72: Data Storytelling**



1\. Data storytelling is the process of presenting data in a meaningful and engaging way.



2\. It combines data, visualizations, and explanations to communicate insights effectively.



3\. A good data story helps people understand trends and make informed decisions.



4\. Charts and graphs are often used to support the story and highlight key findings.



Example:

A sales report can show how sales increased over time and explain the reasons behind the growth.
