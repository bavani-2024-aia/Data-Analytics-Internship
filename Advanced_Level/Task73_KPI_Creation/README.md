**TASK 73: KPI Creation**



1\. KPI stands for Key Performance Indicator.



2\. KPIs are measurable values used to track performance and progress toward goals.



3\. They help organizations evaluate success and identify areas for improvement.



4\. Common KPIs include sales revenue, customer satisfaction, profit margin, and employee productivity.



Example:

A company may use monthly sales revenue as a KPI to measure business growth.
