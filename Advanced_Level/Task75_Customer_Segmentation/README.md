**TASK 75: Customer Segmentation**



1\. Customer segmentation is the process of dividing customers into different groups based on shared characteristics.



2\. It helps businesses understand customer needs and preferences more effectively.



3\. Segmentation can be based on factors such as age, location, purchasing behavior, or interests.



4\. Customer segmentation supports targeted marketing and improves customer satisfaction.



Example:

A company may group customers as Regular Customers, Premium Customers, and New Customers based on their purchase history.
