**TASK 76: Time Series Analysis**



1. Time Series Analysis is the process of analyzing data collected over a period of time.
2. It helps identify trends, patterns, and seasonal variations in data.
3. Businesses use time series analysis for forecasting future sales, demand, and performance.
4. Time series data is recorded at regular intervals such as daily, monthly, or yearly.



Example:

A company analyzes its monthly sales data to predict future sales trends and make better business decisions.
