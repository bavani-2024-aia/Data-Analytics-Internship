&#x20;**TASK 79: A/B Testing**



1\. A/B Testing is a method of comparing two versions of a product, webpage, or feature to determine which performs better.



2\. It helps organizations make data-driven decisions based on user behavior.



3\. In A/B Testing, users are divided into two groups: Group A and Group B.



4\. The performance of both groups is measured and compared using key metrics.



Example:

A company shows two different website designs to users and compares the click rates to identify the better-performing design.
