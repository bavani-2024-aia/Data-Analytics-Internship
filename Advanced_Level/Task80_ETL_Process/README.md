&#x20;**TASK 80: ETL Process**



1\. ETL stands for Extract, Transform, and Load.



2\. It is a process used to collect data from different sources, clean and transform it, and load it into a target system.



3\. ETL helps organizations prepare data for analysis and reporting.



4\. It is widely used in data warehouses and business intelligence systems.



Example:

A company extracts sales data from multiple branches, transforms it into a standard format, and loads it into a central database for reporting.
